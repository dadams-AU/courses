Grading Summary

  • Reading Quizzes (5) — 20% (Fridays, weekly)
  • Policy Problem Proposal — 10% (Fri, May 29)
  • Problem Definition Memo — 15% (Fri, June 5)
  • Alternatives and Evaluation Matrix — 15% (Fri, June 12)
  • Draft Policy Memo — 15% (Sat, June 20)
  • Final Policy Memo — 20% (Fri, June 26)
  • Final Reflection — 5% (Fri, June 26)

Submission Format — Google Docs

All stages of the policy writing project must be drafted and submitted as a single Google Doc that you carry forward across all five weeks. Before your first submission, share the document with dpadams@fullerton.edu as an Editor. Each week, paste the shareable link into the Canvas assignment by the Friday deadline. Microsoft Word, PDF, or other file uploads will not be accepted.
