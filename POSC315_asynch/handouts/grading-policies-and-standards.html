Make-up and Late Submission Policy

All assignments are due by 11:59 p.m. on the date specified in the course schedule. Because of the compressed five-week schedule, late work cannot be accommodated except in cases of documented illness or other unforeseen circumstances. Extensions must be requested in writing before the due date. Reading quizzes cannot be made up.

Alternative Procedures for Submitting Work

All written work in this course is submitted as a Google Doc, with the shareable link posted in the corresponding Canvas assignment by the deadline. If you cannot submit via Canvas or are unable to share the Google Doc, contact the professor immediately to arrange an alternative.

Participation

Because this course is asynchronous, "participation" means timely completion of weekly quizzes and project stages and engagement with instructor feedback on your draft memo. There are no live class sessions to attend.

Extra Credit

There are no extra credit assignments in this course.

Retention of Student Work

Work submitted for a grade through Canvas shall be retained for a reasonable time after the term ends, not to exceed the last day of the subsequent semester. Students have the right to review graded work in the presence of the instructor. (UPS 320.005) Students are also responsible for retaining personal copies of all submitted and returned work.
