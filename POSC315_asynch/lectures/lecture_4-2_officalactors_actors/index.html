Key Takeaways

  • Executive branch wields significant formal and informal power
  • Bureaucracy brings technical expertise and discretionary authority
  • Judiciary shapes policy through constitutional interpretation
  • Policy outcomes emerge from complex interactions among actors
  • Understanding both institutional structures and behavioral incentives is essential
