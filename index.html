POSC 521: MPA Capstone - Public Administration Theory

View Syllabus

Course Overview

This capstone course synthesizes the knowledge and skills developed throughout the MPA program, with a focus on public administration theory and its practical applications.

Students will engage with advanced theoretical frameworks and apply them to real-world administrative challenges, culminating in a comprehensive final project.

The extensive reading list is designed to deepen understanding of core administrative concepts and prepare students for leadership roles in public service.
